Don’t feed your plants chemicals!!!

Chemical fertilizer is beneficial to your plant in the short term, fast cheap nutrients, yumm. However, it is damaging to the soil, and your plant’s habitat, the use of chemical fertilizer will harden soil, reduce soil organic matter and pH, suffocating your plant, stunting your plant’s growth, and eventually killing your plant.

How can you help us?

There are many ways to help us, it does not necessarily mean donating, it can be as simple as sharing our page to raise awareness, we believe many out there still not known of how big a problem food waste is, to our environment; biodiversity loss, climate change, and land wastage to name a few. Nevertheless, here are the ways you could help us:

Donate
